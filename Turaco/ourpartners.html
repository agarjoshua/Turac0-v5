CASE STUDY

Don't just take our word for it...
here's how we've done it before

A little over a year ago, we began a partnership in Kenya with M-KOPA, a connected asset finance platform. Through this collaboration, we launched a bundle product dubbed Hospital Cash & Life. Hospital Cash & Life insurance provides cashback in case of hospital admission, and pays out to the next of kin in the unfortunate event that the insured passes away. The aim was to provide M-KOPA Direct Sales Representatives, customers and their families with a safety net against financial shocks caused by unexpected health risks. Since the partnership began, we have insured over 16,000 lives in Kenya alone, with plans to reach even more people in future

In frame, Victor Risa, M-KOPA Direct Sales Representative in Kenya
